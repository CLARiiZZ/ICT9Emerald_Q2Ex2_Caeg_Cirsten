Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint

Kim Dokja

The main character of the light novel, Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint. He is often described as a morally-grey character because of his decisions in the post-apocalyptic environment he's put in, but in the end, he's shown that he's willing to sacrifice even his own life for the ones he loves. Huge bookworm and is a loyal reader of "Ways of Survival".

Yoo Joonghyuk

The main character of the fictional novel inside the light novel, Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint, that is called "Ways of Survival". In the timeline where the novel started, he's described as a cold, stubborn, and cruel character, one that even Kim Dokja wouldn't want to meet despite being a loyal reader of his novel. Although, he was originally a caring person at heart, but the continuous regressions of his cruel life wore him down and turned him into the way he is now.
